    ABOUT AMY

    I am a certified coach living in Bondi in Sydney, Australia and I have helped over 18,000 women from around the world online to become stronger and more confident.

    My passion for fitness began when I ran up a flight of stairs over 10 years ago and realized that I truly wasn’t fit at all! This lead me to join a gym for the first time... And the rest is truly history!

    Boy have I come a long way, when I think back to that time! Over the years I have tried powerlifting, bodybuilding, CrossFit, a half-marathon, gymnastics, calisthenics and even Circus Sports. I have learned a tonne & have transformed my body into something I never thought it could be.




    Before becoming a coach, I actually worked in advertising & marketing, even studying a Master's Degree before realizing I was crazy not to follow my obvious passion for fitness!

    Even though I had doubts & fears about leaving that path, I eventually followed my passion & took the leap anyway... And fast forward to today I'm helping thousands of women around the world - and I'm loving every minute of it!

    WHAT'S INSIDE?

    Amy's eBooks contain a 12 Week Workout & Meal Plan tailored to your specific goal. Inside, you'll find:

    Less Than 3 Hours Training a Week

    Because we are all busy :) So we need a solution that is fast and effective! You may be wondering, can you really get results in just 3 hours a week? Yes. Absolutely. It's what I do, it's what my clients do, and it works. It's about using your workout time effectively! Instead of spending hours training without structure and a strategy, you will get in - get a fast, fun and effective workout - and get out. In your sessions, you will be focusing on the most important exercises for your goal. And you'll actually be able to stick to them, because you can fit them into your week easily!

    50+ Healthy, 15 Minute Recipes

    Because we all want to eat healthily & cook these perfect, elaborate meals - but who has the time? I know I don't ;) And besides, there are SO many delicious and healthy recipes that can be made in 15 minutes or less! Seriously. I have focused on the recipes that are delicious, healthy and can be made fast! Sure, I could give you lots of fancy recipes which would require you to buy lots of fancy ingredients and spend hours in the kitchen - but your goal is to get in shape & enjoy it, not become a michelin star chef! ;) Instead, the recipes are tasty, speedy & easy.

    An Exercise Library with Photos

    Because sometimes, you just need to see someone do it! Or read a few cues to help you to get the hang of a certain movement.

    Home & Gym Versions Included

    If you can't afford a gym, have kids to look after or travel a lot - there's a 12 week Home Version included in every program, as well as a 12 Week Gym Version - so gym or no gym, you'll have an effective and simple plan you can follow.

    Special Diet Options

    Are you following a Vegan or Vegetarian Diet? Are you cutting out Gluten or Dairy? I have catered my plans to you! Many of my recipes are already Vegan, Vegetarian, GF or dairy-free - but even when they aren't, I have included a delicious substitute ingredient! You might already know, I am a big lover of plant-based eating! So you'll find a lot of my meals are high in fruit & veg. Whatever your diet, there are options for you.

    A 22 Tip Motivation Guide

    For some women, motivation is the biggest challenge! It's no good if I just provide you with a diet & a workout program - you need to master your mind! I have shared here how I get and stay motivated (and believe me, it's not something I was born with!). My tips are practical & I even cover common obstacles & excuses (like sickness, money, injuries, motivation & more) and how to overcome these challenges! You won't be missing workouts anymore after you follow these tips in my eBook.
