    ABOUT AMY

    I help women achieve their dream body. I am a certified coach living in Bondi in Sydney, Australia and I have helped over 18,000 women from around the world online to become stronger and more confident. My love of health and fitness began over 10 years ago when I joined a gym for the first time...

    Boy have I come a long way, when I think back to that time! Over the years I have tried powerlifting, bodybuilding, CrossFit, a half-marathon, gymnastics, calisthenics and even Circus Sports. I have learned a tonne & have transformed my body into something I never thought it could be.

    Before becoming a coach, I actually spent many years at a desk job, even studying a Bachelor's Degree, and a Master's Degree, before deciding to take the leap and follow my true passion of health and fitness. Fast forward to today and I'm helping thousands of women around the world - and I'm loving every minute of it! :)
