    ABOUT AMY

    I am a certified coach living in Sydney, Australia. I used to work in the corporate world and even studied a Masters Degree before realising I was leading a double life! In my spare time I was living and breathing all things health & fitness. Over the years I had tried powerlifting, bodybuilding, CrossFit, a half-marathon, gymnastics, calisthenics and even Circus Sports, and devoured scientific reports about health and fitness just for fun.


    Eventually, I came to my senses - and became a certified Fitness Coach! Ever since, I've been helping thousands women from around the world online to transform their bodies!
